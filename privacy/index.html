Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 10, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Yalla Traffic ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("Yalla Traffic" or the "App").

By using Yalla Traffic, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use the App.

Our Commitment: Yalla Traffic is designed to help you plan your trips, not to track or monitor you. We only collect location data when necessary to provide traffic and routing services, and we never sell your personal data to third parties.

Information We Collect

1. Location Data

Yalla Traffic requires access to your device's location to provide traffic information and route planning services. We collect:

  • Current Location: Your device's GPS coordinates to show nearby traffic conditions and calculate routes from your current position.
  • Saved Locations: Addresses you save as "Home," "Work," or other favorites are stored locally on your device.

Important: Location data is only collected when you are actively using the App. We do not track your location in the background unless you explicitly enable background location for departure time notifications (optional feature).

2. Usage Data

We may automatically collect certain information when you use the App, including:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App usage statistics (which features you use)
  • Crash reports and performance data
  • Time zone and general region (for time-based features)

3. Saved Places

When you save locations (Home, Work, favorites), this data is stored locally on your device using secure encrypted storage. We do not upload your saved places to our servers.

How We Use Your Information

Data Type Purpose Storage
Current Location Calculate routes, show live traffic, provide ETAs Processed in real-time, not stored on servers
Saved Places Quick access to frequent destinations Stored locally on your device only
Usage Analytics Improve app performance and features Aggregated, anonymized data
Crash Reports Fix bugs and improve stability Anonymized technical data

Third-Party Services

Yalla Traffic uses the following third-party services to provide our features:

TomTom

We use TomTom APIs for traffic data, routing, and place search. When you request traffic information or routes, your location data is sent to TomTom's servers to process the request. TomTom processes this data according to their Privacy Policy.

Analytics

We may use analytics services to understand how users interact with our App. This data is anonymized and aggregated to help us improve the user experience.

Data Retention

  • Location Data: Not stored on our servers. Processed in real-time and discarded.
  • Saved Places: Stored on your device until you delete them or uninstall the App.
  • Analytics Data: Retained for up to 24 months in aggregated, anonymized form.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure local storage on your device
  • Regular security assessments
  • Limited data collection (privacy by design)

Your Rights and Choices

Location Permissions

You can control location access through your device settings at any time:

  • iOS: Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Location Services ‚Üí Yalla Traffic
  • Android: Settings ‚Üí Apps ‚Üí Yalla Traffic ‚Üí Permissions ‚Üí Location

Deleting Your Data

Since most data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it by:

  • Clearing saved places within the App settings
  • Uninstalling the App (removes all local data)

UAE Data Protection

We are committed to complying with UAE data protection requirements. Your data is processed in accordance with:

  • UAE Federal Decree-Law No. 45 of 2021 on Personal Data Protection
  • Relevant regulations issued by the UAE Data Office

For users in the UAE, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data by contacting us at the email address below.

Children's Privacy

Yalla Traffic is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy
